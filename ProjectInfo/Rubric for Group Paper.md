This rubric is intended to help standardizing the evaluation of the final assignment (report/paper) of this course, making
grading  more  consistent  and  transparent  for  students.  Also  it  will  give  some  proper  feedback  to  students  when
communicating the grade. And it will let the students know beforehand what aspects they will be evaluated on, possibly
for a critical self-evaluation of their own work.
The assignment is a multi-dimensional project to be carried out in groups of 2 to 3 students. As a group, students will first
define a flow problem (approval needed), which involves at least two components of heat transfer, chemical reaction, and
turbulence. They will then numerically solve this flow problem using the supplied code or a self-written code. With the same
group they will finally write it out as a scientific paper following the supplied template.
